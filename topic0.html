What is Hacking

Hacking is the practice of gaining the unauthorized aceess of a system, in order to viewing, editing, copying the information or to accomplish a goal outside of the creator's original purpose. The person who is consistently engaging in hacking activities is called a hacker.


Hacker is a term used by some to mean "a clever programmer" and by others, especially those in popular media, to mean "someone who tries to break into computer systems.
